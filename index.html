About QUIZZICLES

QUIZZICLES is an annual mathematics and physics competition designed to challenge and inspire young minds. Our competition targets students from Classes 8-12 and undergraduate 1st year, providing them with a platform to showcase their analytical and problem-solving skills.

We believe in nurturing scientific temperament and mathematical thinking among students through carefully curated questions that test both conceptual understanding and application skills.
